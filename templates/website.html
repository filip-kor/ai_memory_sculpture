MEMORY SCULPTURE GENERATOR

Generate your own unique 3D printed sculpture based on emotions extracted from your describtion of an event, situation, or a nostalgic memory.

Put it on your cupboard like a framed photograph and reach for the moments worth remembering whenever you feel like it!

How to use it?

  1. Record a 30-second voice memo of a moment you want to remember.
  2. Upload it using the "Select file" button below.
  3. Click the "Generate sculpture" button and wait for your sculpture to generate.
  4. Print the sculpture yourself or send it to a 3D printing service.
  5. Reach out for it anytime you'd like to remind yourself of the event.
